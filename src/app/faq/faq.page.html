FAQ
What is Hikma360?
Hikma360 is an information app for halal and non-halal consumers. It allows users to check if a product is halal or not through the search halal feature. It also shows users their favourite grocery stores that they visit over a period of time through the favourites feature.
Do I need to create an account?
You do not need to create an account unless you plan to use the favourites feature.
How do I use the favourites feature?
To use the favourites feature, you must create an account. After this, you can add a grocery store that you have visited by clicking the add button at the buttom right of the screen. Add in the items you purchased and fill out the requirements. For this feature to work, you must enter multiple grocery stores.
