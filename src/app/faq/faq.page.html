FAQ
What is Hikma360?
Hikma360 is an information app for halal and non-halal consumers. We do that with the following features: A "search halal" feature allows you to check if a food item, product or ingredient is halal or not. A "favourites store" feature that shows you the most desirable grocery stores to purchase a specified food item, over a period of time, based on information entered by you that is analyzed by our algorithm. A closet halal "restaurant/grocery store" feature that shows you the closest halal restaurants/grocery stores to you. We currently only offer this feature for those in the GTA. A "list of certifying bodies" feature that shows you the list of halal certification bodies in Cananda and information on their certifying standards as well as the brands they've certified! Finally, we provide information on the Halal dietary guidelines in our "Halal 101" feature that clarifies misconceptions about halal.
Do I need to create an account?
Creating an account is necessary, especially if you intend on using the favourites feature.
How do I use the favourites feature?
To use the favourites feature, you must create an account. After this, you can add a grocery store that you have visited by clicking the add button at the bottom right of the screen. Add in the items you purchased and fill out the requirements. For this feature to work, you must enter multiple grocery stores.
